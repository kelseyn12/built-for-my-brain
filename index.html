the story

built by someone who needed them

I'm Kelsey. I'm a UGC creator, web designer, and musician — and I was recently diagnosed with ADHD in the middle of trying to build a business.

I tried every productivity system. They all assumed things my brain can't do reliably — like consistent self-initiation, accurate time estimation, and not losing the entire thread when life interrupted.

So I started building my own tools. Simple ones. Ones that work even when I disappear for a week. Ones that meet me where I actually am instead of where I'm supposed to be.

Everything here is free. Because I wish I'd found something like this earlier.

"traditional productivity systems fail ADHD adults because they require consistent self-initiation and maintenance. the tools that work are the ones that adapt to your energy state."

— ADHD productivity research, 2025
